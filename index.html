About

Tour De Shred fest is an earth day music festival celebrating the bay area music and arts scene. Our goal is to have a multitude of bands spread out at three different venues throughout the day. We want as many people as possible to walk or bike to and from each venue in celebration of earth day. We plan on having as many local businesses and artists contribute to the fest as well by having booths or tents at each venue.
